  • Holding a number 1-4 and clicking a cell jumps to a particular velocity.
  • Holding shift and hovering over cells will delete them.
  • Holding shift & then a number 1-4 and hovering over cells with set them to that velocity.
  • Hit the space bar to restart the machine.
  • Hit 'r' over and over again to get more slapback reverb. (Shift+'r' to get less.)
  • Hit 'v' over and over again to get more volume. (Shift+'v' to get less.)
